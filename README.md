## 🧩 About Me

I'm a **Data Scientist** completing a professional specialization in **Statistical Analytics**, with a background in Biology that gave me a strong investigative foundation. I now apply that discipline to the complex data architectures of the financial world.

My focus is **Risk Analysis** for the **Banking and Fintech sectors** — building predictive models that are not only accurate but *explainable*, so stakeholders can act on them with confidence.

- 🏦 Specializing in **Churn Prediction**, **Credit Risk**, and **Financial Inclusion** analytics
- 🔬 Background: Biology → Data Science (the pattern-recognition skills transfer perfectly)
- 🇨🇴 Currently building a Colombian fintech/insurtech project series using real national datasets
- 📊 Strong emphasis on **Explainable AI** with SHAP — models that tell you *why*, not just *what*
- 🎯 Goal: Data Scientist role in Risk or Analytics within Banking or Fintech

## 📂 Featured Projects

### 🏦 [Customer Churn Prediction & Explainable AI — Beta Bank](https://github.com/Juanhv24/Beta-Bank)
Predicting which bank customers are likely to churn using **Random Forest + SMOTE** on an imbalanced dataset. SHAP analysis identifies the key drivers: age, activity status, and geography.

| Metric | Score |
|--------|-------|
| F1 Score | **0.62** |
| AUC-ROC | **0.86** |
| Models tested | 3 |

`Python` `Scikit-Learn` `Random Forest` `SHAP` `SMOTE` `Imbalanced-learn`

---

### 🚗 Vehicle Insurance Claims — EDA *(In Development)*
Exploratory analysis of Colombia's mandatory vehicle insurance (**SOAT**) claims data from Fasecolda. Mapping accident frequency, claim severity, and regional patterns across departments.

`Python` `Pandas` `Matplotlib` `Seaborn`

---

### 📊 Financial Inclusion Gap Analysis — Colombia *(Upcoming)*
Statistical testing on Banca de las Oportunidades data to evaluate whether rural location and income level are significant predictors of being unbanked in Colombia.

`Python` `SciPy` `Hypothesis Testing` `Statsmodels`

---

### 🏢 SME Credit Risk — Informal Economy *(Upcoming)*
Classification model predicting which informal Colombian businesses are likely to access formal credit — built on DANE's Encuesta de Micronegocios. Directly relevant to fintech lending.

`Python` `XGBoost` `Scikit-Learn` `SHAP`
